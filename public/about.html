About

Hey there! My name's Karan Dahiya and this little website is a digital sketchbook to host my attempts at learning to draw figures, portraits, and little doodles here and there as a break from my studies. I'm a computer engineering student at the University of Waterloo and I started learning to draw as a creative outlet and a break from academics.

I've still got a long way to go until I can produce anything half decent, but I think it'll be interesting to be able to scroll through and look back on my progress as I keep drawing and (hopefully) improving over time. Thanks for visiting!

- Karan Dahiya
